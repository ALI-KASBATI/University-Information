Welcome to PAF-KIET University

PAF-KIET University is a prestigious institution of higher education located in Karachi, Pakistan. Established in 2000, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in engineering, management, computer science, and social sciences.

Our mission is to provide quality education and research opportunities to our students, and to prepare them for leadership roles in their chosen fields. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation, creativity, and intellectual curiosity, and to promoting social responsibility and ethical values.
